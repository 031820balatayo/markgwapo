About Me

Hey there, I'm Mark Reynald Larida Balatayo, originally from Punta Cruz, Maribojoc in Bohol. But guess what? I was actually born in the vibrant city of Cebu. Coffee? Oh, it's my ultimate energy booster and happiness-inducer. Can't resist its magical powers! When I'm not sipping on a cup of joe, you'll probably find me on the field playing football, basketball, or frisbee. I also have a creative side, so I enjoy creating art. Oh, and let's not forget about my adorable lobsters—I take great care of them too! Now, here's something interesting about me: I tend to be a bit of an introvert when I meet new folks. It takes me a little time to warm up. But hey, once I settle in and get comfortable, I transform into a total social butterfly! About four years ago, my family made a move here. Why? Well, simply because it's where my dad wanted us to be. He's originally from this area, and my mom comes from Iloilo. Now, get this—we're quite the lively bunch. I have five awesome brothers and three amazing sisters. Adventure is practically in our DNA! We love exploring new horizons and doing our part to protect the environment. Plus, lending a helping hand to those in need is something that truly warms our hearts.
